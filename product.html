Types Of Dongle

Original Dongle

Original Dongle is one of our best selling Dongo and has an everchanging recipe. Each generation of dongle has changed according to what kingdom's borders were covering our shop and currently we are inside the boreders of roland. Our recipe to make Dongle has always stayed the same the only changes are in our secret batter. We make Dongle by shaping our batter to different size balls and cooking them part way, then dipping them in the candy coating, pink is Strawberry, green is Apple, and yellow is Lemon. After they are coated we put a stick through them and put them back in the oven, to cook fully through.

Peppered Dongle

Peppered Dongle is made all the same as Normal Dongle, but, instead of just putting the sick in we roll them all in pepper, then cook them through.

Sparkled Dongle

Sprakled Dongle is not actually made of Sparkles but is sprinkled with rock salt. Sparkled Dongle is one of our better selling recipes so we urge yout to try some.

Choclate Chip Dongle

Choclate Chip Dongle is our batter, candy coating and the choclate chips all mixed together and using magic we bring the choclate chips to sthe surface while cooking and moving the coating to fill in those spaces.
